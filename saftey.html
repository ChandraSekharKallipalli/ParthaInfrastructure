Site Safety Guidelines

Introduction:

Welcome to the Site Safety Guidelines section. At Partha Infrastructures, we prioritize safety during every phase of construction. Here, we outline essential safety rules and guidelines followed on our construction sites to ensure a secure working environment.

1. Construction site access:

  • Identification: Hazardous materials are clearly identified, and proper storage and handling protocols are strictly followed.
  • Visitor Check-In: Visitors must check in and wear designated safety gear while on-site.

2. Hazardous Materials:

  • Authorized Personnel: Only authorized personnel are allowed entry to construction sites.
  • Visitor Check-In: Visitors must check in and wear designated safety gear while on-site.

3. Fall Protection:

  • Personal Fall Arrest Systems: Workers at elevated heights use personal fall arrest systems.
  • Guardrails and Safety Nets: Open edges and floor openings are protected with guardrails or safety nets.

Structural Safety Guidelines

Introduction:

In this section, we focus on the Structural Safety Guidelines followed at Partha Infrastructures to ensure the stability and integrity of our construction projects.

1. Routine Maintenance:

  • Scheduled Inspections: All construction equipment undergoes routine inspections to identify and address maintenance needs.
  • Operator Training: Operators are trained to recognize potential issues and report them promptly.

2. Safety Features:

  • Emergency Shut-off: All equipment is equipped with emergency shut-off mechanisms for quick deactivation in case of emergencies.
  • Safety Signage: Clear and visible safety signage indicating proper usage and potential hazards.

3. Risk Management:

  • Risk Assessments: Regular risk assessments are conducted to identify and mitigate potential hazards associated with equipment operation.
  • Reporting Mechanism: A robust reporting system is in place for workers to report any equipment safety concerns.
